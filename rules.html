Objective

Find the Imposter. Everyone knows the topic except them!!

How to Play

  1. Pass the device around. Each player sees the topic. The Imposter sees only a Hint.
  2. Each player goes around and says a word related to the topic.
  3. Vote on who you think the Imposter is.
  4. If the Imposter is caught, they lose! However if the Imposter is not caught or the guess the topic, all the players lose!

Tips

  • Give subtle clues if you know the Topic; avoid being too obvious.
  • Losers of each round may drink for more fun
